About FitX

FitX-Our mision and vision.


In today's World, nearly every activity is just as physically demanding as it is mentally. So, maintaining a healthy physique is not a fashion statement anymore, it has become a necessity.
Our goal is to provide a compact weekly workout schedule which you can incorporate into your busy everyday lives easily and embark on your journey towards a healthy physique.

Our objectives :

  • Setting an achievable target for each day so you can work your way up to the bigger goal.
  • More focus on 'at home' workouts so you don't need heavy machinery and can still stay in shape.
  • Achieving consistency through light working, but daily working.
